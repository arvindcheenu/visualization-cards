About ViBBS

Visualization Building Blocks and Scenarios

The ViBBS – Visualization Building Blocks and Scenarios tool helps you explore and organize different visualization techniques and design patterns. Each card represents a specific approach or method that can be applied to data visualization projects.

Cards courtesy of Thorsten May from Fraunhofer IGD, Lena Cibulski from the University of Rostock, Peter Musaeus and Hans-Jörg Schulz from Aarhus University. Developed by Arvind Srinivasan as a personal project to make these digital.

How to Use

  • Pin cards by clicking on them or using the pin button to save them to your collection
  • Hide pinned cards in each category to focus on unpinned options
  • Filter pinned cards by category in the bottom drawer
  • Open the drawer at the bottom to view and manage your pinned cards
  • Switch to Scenarios to view scenario gallery with PhotoSwipe lightbox
